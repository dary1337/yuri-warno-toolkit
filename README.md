# Yuri's WARNO Toolkit

The successor to the **YSM Installer**: it still installs **YSM** and its collaborations in one click
— and adds a full **battlegroup editor** that works directly on your WARNO profile.

> ### 🔒 Closed beta
> The toolkit is in **closed testing** — not for public release yet.
> **Want in?** Request access on the YSM Community Discord: **<https://discord.gg/seVVnBcsuS>**

This repository holds the public **mod catalog** (`mods-list.json`) and mod icons the app reads.

## What it does
- **Installs the mods** — YSM, WTO, YSM × WiF, YSM × WiF × WTO. Finds your WARNO (Steam or non-Steam), picks the build that matches your game version, and installs it safely — your current setup is backed up first and rolled back if anything fails.
- **Edits your battlegroups** — reads every deck in your profile, *including the ones WARNO hides*
  (built under another mod or an older game build). Edit units, veterancy and transports; rename,
  delete, duplicate or copy a deck to the other coalition; or build new battlegroups from scratch.
  Changes are written straight back into the game profile — always backed up and verified first.
- **Profile backups** — snapshot and restore your whole profile; every change is reversible.
